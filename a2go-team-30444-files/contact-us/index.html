Joining the Team

We are actively looking for students who are passionate about science & engineering, business, or the arts, and interested in a highly competitive robotics experience. We would be happy to talk with you and see if our team would be a good fit, or otherwise help to connect you with other robotics teams that might be a good fit for you! There's a place for everyone in FIRST, whether it's around technical, creating, outreach, or other elements. Please reach out to us, we look forward to talking with you!
