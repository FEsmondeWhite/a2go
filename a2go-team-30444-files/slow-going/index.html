Tuesday practice

Francis E-W November 4, 2025

Because of the Nov 4 election, Ann Arbor schools were closed. So our team had a great afternoon practice at Tech Workshop. We set up PedroPathing, tuned our robot (which weighs 30 pounds!), replaced a servo that had broken, and learned how to use Pedro Pathing to create great auto sequences. Thanks to Tom May, Tech Workshop, and FTC team 28804 (the Cybersmiths) who have helped us with code, looking into mechanical trouble, and field practice.

The team has a lot to get done before the upcoming qualifier in Midland! I can't wait to see the results.
