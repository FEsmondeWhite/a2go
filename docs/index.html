A2GO Robotics

Our Ann Arbor based rookie team of middle school students is learning to design and build a robot for competing in the First Tech Challenge robotics competition. We will compete in at least two FTC competitions in Michigan in 2025/2026. In each competition we compete against, but also collaborate with, many other teams.

In FTC we build a robot to score points in games where the rules change each year. The theme of the upcoming 2025/2026 FIRST competition is First Age, with the competitions themed around archaeology. The FTC challenge will be called Decode; the rules will be released on September 6th. In FTC points are scored by accomplishing autonomous and driver-controlled objectives (like putting blocks into scoring boxes, or having the robot climb scaffolding).
