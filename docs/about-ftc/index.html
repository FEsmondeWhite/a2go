About FTC

What is First Tech Challenge (FTC)?

FTC, also known as First Tech Challenge, is a global robotics competition. The competition is run by the FIRST organization, which aims to help kids learn more about STEM.

Every year, a new FTC competition is released in September. Teams across Michigan design, build, and compete with their robots September through December, with 8 state champions advancing to the worldâ€™s competitions the following spring.

There are over 500 FTC teams in Michigan, with members in grades 6-8 (middle school). Many middle schools in Washtenaw County have teams! So, if you want to get more hands-on and involved in STEM, or just want to have fun, know that this is a place to do it!
