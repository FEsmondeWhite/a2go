Sponsorship

Our team is currently looking for sponsors to help offset the costs of running our team, pledging future donations should we advance to the international competition, provide in-kind materials and resources, and to provide mentorship opportunities (like in person or remote meetings) to our team members.

This year we have already traveled to competition in Midland, and we will be traveling to the States Northwest competition in Muskegon. We also aim to advance from the State level competition to compete at the World competition in Houston in the spring.

Please contact Francis Esmonde-White (francis@esmonde-white.com, 734-546-5481) for more information.

Our Sponsors
